Lesson Summary

Lesson 3 emphasizes progression: repeating exercises from Lesson 2 with increased frequency, and introducing comparative analysis across multiple days of logs.

Guidance: Pay attention to consistencies across extended casting. Mark differences clearly and avoid bias in interpretation.

Protocol

  • Increase casting sessions to 12 times per day for 7 days.
  • Continue documenting patterns and seniority.
  • Compare logs at the end of each day for trends.

Daily Practice

  • Note time, environment, and mood.
  • Highlight repeated Odu or pairings.
  • Share insights during weekly review.

Daily Submission

Email your daily log (photo of cast + notes) to Baba@swamiginstitute.com. Include:

  • Date, time, and session number
  • Cast patterns, seniority notes, and reflections
  • Summary of observed trends across the day
