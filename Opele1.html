Lesson Summary

This lesson establishes the energetic baseline for your practice. Keep your mat, Opélé, and body position consistent; follow your opening; and begin documenting each cast with time, pattern, and brief guidance statements.

Tip: If your Opele1.pub contains specific affirmations, invocations, or diagrams, export them as images and replace the placeholders below.

Protocol

  • Prepare the mat and Opélé; observe opening sequence.
  • Cast to establish energy; note pattern and initial guidance.
  • Repeat in a steady rhythm to build familiarity.
  • Write concise notes immediately after each cast.

Daily Practice

  • Commit to a consistent time and space.
  • Capture photos or sketches of key casts.
  • Record conditions that may influence results.
  • Keep entries short and truthful; avoid leading prompts.
