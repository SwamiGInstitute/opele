Lesson Summary

  • Cast Opélé once to establish the dominant energy.
  • Cast a second time to determine the seniority of the first cast (first senior = “+”, otherwise “–”).
  • Cast 8 times a day for 8 consecutive days while establishing seniority.
  • Document all findings and submit daily according to schedule.

Supported by Heru(+) Ogbe.

Protocol

  • Open, center, and cast once — record the dominant energy.
  • Cast again — if the first is senior to the second, mark “+”; else “–”.
  • Repeat in a calm, consistent rhythm.

Daily Practice

  • 8 casts per day for 8 straight days.
  • Keep concise, objective notes (time, pattern, seniority sign).
  • Email your log and photo each day per schedule.

Submission

Send your daily log (cast photo + notes) to Baba@swamiginstitute.com.
