Lesson Summary

Mark each Odu using single or double dots from right to left. Cast Opélé four times per day for ten consecutive days, marking Odu and noting the Ibi/Ire character of the first pair relative to the second.

Exercise: Determine seniority between first and second Odu pairs. Document results faithfully.
